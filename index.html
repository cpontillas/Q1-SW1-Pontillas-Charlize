Why I Love Metal

Metal is a subgenre of rock and roll, getting its name from how its music is like.
Heavy metal is usually characterized by loud distortion, electric guitars, strong repetitive rhythms, dense bass-drums, and vigorous vocals.

My Favorite Metal Band

My favorite metal band of all time is The Band Ghost.
Ghost is a Swedish metal band that currently consists of 9 members, including their lead vocalist, Tobias Forge, better known as Papa Emeritus.
This band is my favorite because, like many others, I find their on-stage presence unique.
Ghost presents their members in a way that the identities of them are hidden under matching, face-covered costumes and are called 'Nameless Ghouls'.
Besides that, the band also has an interesting backstory behind their frontman, why he is Papa Emeritus, a character like a 'demonic anti-pope'.

This is the band during their 3rd album release.
As seen, their frontman is still Tobias Forge, though he plays the character Papa Emeritus III or Terzo, instead of Papa Emeritus IV (the current one).
All of the "Papa"'s are brothers, their mother being Sister Imperator (deceased), the Head of the Nameless Ghouls, and their father being Papa Nihil (deceased),
the Head of the Clergy which is basically what the band is called in their backstory/lore.
Though with each album, there is a different one (minus Copia/Papa IV since he had two) because they are killed off after producing their album.
